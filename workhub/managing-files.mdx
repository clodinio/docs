# Folders

Folders in WorkHub allow you to organise your recordings and transcripts by project, study, client, or any structure that suits your workflow.

## Creating a folder

1. Navigate to **WorkHub** in the sidebar
2. Click **New Folder**
3. Give the folder a name and confirm

## Navigating folders

Click any folder to open it and view its contents. You can navigate back using the breadcrumb trail at the top of the screen.

## Folder contents

Each folder can contain:

- Audio and video recordings
- Completed transcripts associated with those recordings

## Organising your workspace

We recommend naming folders consistently, for example:

- By project: `Project Alpha`, `Study 2024`
- By client: `Client Name — Q3 Research`
- By research phase: `Recruitment Interviews`, `Follow-up Sessions`

<Tip>
  A clear folder structure makes it much easier to locate transcripts when you're deep into analysis. Set it up at the start of each project rather than reorganising later.
</Tip>
