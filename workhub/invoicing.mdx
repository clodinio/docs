## How It Works

1. Your team uploads recordings and requests transcription through WorkHub as usual
2. Work is completed and returned to your workspace
3. Our team invoices your organization at the agreed billing interval (monthly, per project, or per milestone)
4. Payment is made against the invoice by bank transfer, ACH, or institutional purchase order

## Billing Intervals

Billing intervals are set when your WorkHub account is configured. Common arrangements include:

- Monthly invoicing for ongoing programs
- Per-project invoicing for discrete studies
- Milestone-based invoicing for phased research programs

## Payment Methods for WorkHub Accounts

WorkHub accounts support:

- Institutional purchase orders with Net 30 payment terms
- ACH bank transfers
- Wire transfers
- Direct invoicing to departmental or grant accounts

Late payments on Net 30 terms are subject to a 1.5% monthly interest charge per our Terms of Service.

## Institutional Purchase Orders

We accept purchase orders and provide itemized invoices, W-9 forms, and service summaries to support your organization's procurement and accounts payable processes. We have completed vendor onboarding for multiple universities, hospital systems, and enterprise organizations.

To set up purchase order billing or provide procurement documentation, contact [support@qualtranscribe.com](mailto:support@qualtranscribe.com).

## Order History and Audit Trail

All transcription orders associated with your WorkHub account are visible in the **Orders** section of your account. This provides a complete record for invoice reconciliation, grant reporting, and internal audit purposes.

## Questions About an Invoice

For invoice inquiries, contact [support@qualtranscribe.com](mailto:support@qualtranscribe.com) with your account name, invoice number, and the relevant date range. We respond to billing inquiries within one business day.
