# Invoicing

WorkHub accounts operate on a postpaid, invoiced billing model. Rather than paying per upload at checkout, your transcription work is tracked and invoiced manually at agreed intervals.

## How it works

1. Your team uploads recordings and requests transcriptions through WorkHub as normal
2. Completed work is tracked against your account
3. Our team issues an invoice at your agreed billing interval (monthly, per project, etc.)
4. Payment is made against the invoice by bank transfer or agreed method

## Setting up invoiced billing

Invoicing terms are agreed when your WorkHub account is set up. To discuss pricing and billing arrangements, contact [support@qualtranscribe.com](mailto:support@qualtranscribe.com).

## Viewing order history

All transcription orders associated with your WorkHub account are visible in your **Orders** section. This provides a full audit trail for reconciling invoices.

<Note>
  WorkHub invoicing is managed manually. If you have questions about a specific invoice, contact our support team with your account name and the relevant date range.
</Note>
