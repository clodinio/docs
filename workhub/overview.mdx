# WorkHub

WorkHub is a secure, folder-based workspace designed for postpaid and institutional clients. It gives research teams a dedicated space to organise recordings, access transcripts, and manage projects — with manual invoicing rather than per-upload payments.

## Who is WorkHub for?

WorkHub is designed for:

- Research institutions and universities
- Market research agencies
- Healthcare organisations
- Any client with ongoing, high-volume transcription needs who prefers invoiced billing

## Getting access

WorkHub access is set up manually by our team. To get started, contact us at [support@qualtranscribe.com](mailto:support@qualtranscribe.com) and we'll arrange your workspace setup.

<Note>
  WorkHub is not available to standard prepaid accounts. It requires a separate account configuration.
</Note>
