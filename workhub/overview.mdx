## Who WorkHub Is For

WorkHub is built for:

- Research institutions and university departments managing multi-phase projects
- Market research agencies running parallel studies
- Law firms and litigation support teams with ongoing deposition workflows
- Healthcare systems and CROs managing clinical interview programs
- Pharmaceutical companies running multi-site studies
- Public health and international development organizations
- Consulting firms and corporate operations teams
- Any organization processing recordings at sustained volume

## How WorkHub Is Different from Prepaid

With a prepaid account, each order is placed and paid individually at checkout. This works well for individuals and organizations with occasional or unpredictable transcription needs.

WorkHub removes the per-order payment step. Your team uploads recordings into a shared project workspace, work is processed and returned to the same workspace, and billing is handled through invoiced arrangements at agreed intervals.

This structure supports larger teams, reduces administrative friction on high-volume projects, and enables institutional procurement processes.
