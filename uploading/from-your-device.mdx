# Upload from Your Device

The most direct way to submit a recording is to upload it from your device. Files are transferred over an encrypted connection (TLS 1.3) and stored on secure, regionally isolated infrastructure.

## How to Upload

**Prepaid accounts:**

1. From your dashboard, click **Order Transcription**
2. Click **Choose File** or drag and drop your file into the upload zone
3. Select your service (Human Transcription or Instant Draft)
4. Configure your add-ons and turnaround
5. Review your order and complete payment

**WorkHub:**

1. Navigate to **WorkHub** in the sidebar
2. Open or create the relevant project folder
3. Click **Upload** within the folder
4. Select your file and confirm

## Supported Formats

MP3, MP4, WAV, M4A, and WebM. See [Supported File Formats](/transcription/supported-formats) for full details.

## File Size

For files larger than 2GB, contact support before uploading as handling may differ from standard uploads.

<Tip>
  You can also import recordings directly from Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, Box, Zoom, or Webex without downloading them to your device first. See the relevant upload guide for each service.
</Tip>
