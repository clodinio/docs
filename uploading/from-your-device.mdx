# Upload from Your Device

The simplest way to submit a file is to upload it directly from your computer or mobile device.

## How to upload

1. From your dashboard, click **New Transcription**
2. In the upload panel, click **Choose File** or drag and drop your file into the upload zone
3. Select your service (Human Transcription or Instant Draft)
4. Configure your add-ons and turnaround (Human Transcription only)
5. Review your order and complete payment

## Supported formats

MP3, MP4, WAV, M4A, WebM. See the full [Supported File Formats](/transcription/supported-formats) page for details.

## Large files

If your file is over 2GB, contact [support@qualtranscribe.com](mailto:support@qualtranscribe.com) before uploading.

<Tip>
  You can also import files from Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, Box, Zoom, or Webex if your recording is already in the cloud.
</Tip>
