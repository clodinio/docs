## Connecting Box

1. From the upload panel, click **Box**
2. Sign in to your Box account when prompted
3. Grant Qualtranscribe read access to your files
4. Your Box account will open in the file picker

## Selecting a File

Browse your folders and select the recording you want to transcribe. Click **Select** to add it to your order.

## Permissions

Qualtranscribe requests read-only access to your Box files. We cannot modify, delete, or create files in your account.

<Note>
  You only need to connect Box once. Your connection is remembered for future orders.
</Note>
