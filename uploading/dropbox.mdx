# Import from Dropbox

You can import recordings directly from your Dropbox account without downloading them to your device first.

## Connecting Dropbox

1. From the upload panel, click **Dropbox**
2. Sign in to your Dropbox account when prompted
3. Grant Qualtranscribe read access to your files
4. Your Dropbox account will open in the file picker

## Selecting a File

Browse your folders and select the recording you want to transcribe. Click **Select** to add it to your order.

## Permissions

Qualtranscribe requests read-only access to your Dropbox files. We cannot modify, delete, or create files in your account.

<Note>
  You only need to connect Dropbox once. Your connection is remembered for future orders.
</Note>
