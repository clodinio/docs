# Import from Dropbox

You can import audio and video files directly from your Dropbox account without downloading them to your device first.

## Connecting Dropbox

1. From the upload panel, click **Dropbox**
2. Sign in to your Dropbox account when prompted
3. Grant Qualtranscribe permission to access your files
4. Your Dropbox account will open in the file picker

## Selecting a file

Browse your folders and select the file you want to transcribe. Click **Select** to add it to your order.

<Note>
  You only need to connect Dropbox once. Your connection will be remembered for future orders.
</Note>
