# Import from Webex

You can import Webex cloud recordings directly into Qualtranscribe for transcription.

## Before You Import

Webex recordings must be saved to Webex cloud to be importable. Recordings are accessible from your Webex account portal once processing is complete, typically within 30-60 minutes after the session ends.

## Connecting Webex

1. From the upload panel, click **Webex**
2. Sign in to your Webex account when prompted
3. Grant Qualtranscribe access to your cloud recordings
4. Your Webex recordings will appear in the file picker

## Selecting a Recording

Browse your recordings and select the one you want to transcribe. Webex recordings are typically available in MP4 format.

## Recording Quality

For best transcription results from Webex sessions:

- Ask participants to use headphones to reduce feedback and echo
- Record in a quiet environment and encourage participants to mute when not speaking
- Use a wired connection where possible to reduce audio compression artifacts

<Tip>
  Webex is commonly used in enterprise and healthcare environments where security matters. Qualtranscribe's HIPAA-compliant workflows and GDPR-aligned infrastructure make it well suited for transcribing sensitive Webex content including clinical meetings, stakeholder sessions, and compliance reviews.
</Tip>
