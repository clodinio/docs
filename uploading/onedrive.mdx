---
title: "Import from Onedrive"
description: "How to connect Onedrive and import audio or video recordings for transcription without downloading them to your device."
---

## Connecting Onedrive

1. From the upload panel, click **Onedrive**
2. Sign in to your Onedrive account when prompted
3. Grant Qualtranscribe read access to your files
4. Your Onedrive account will open in the file picker

## Selecting a File

Browse your folders and select the recording you want to transcribe. Click **Select** to add it to your order.

## Permissions

Qualtranscribe requests read-only access to your Onedrive files. We cannot modify, delete, or create files in your account.

<Note>
  You only need to connect Onedrive once. Your connection is remembered for future orders.
</Note>
