# Import from Google Drive

You can import audio and video files directly from your Google Drive without downloading them to your device first.

## Connecting Google Drive

1. From the upload panel, click **Google Drive**
2. Sign in with your Google account when prompted
3. Grant Qualtranscribe permission to access your Drive files
4. Your Drive will open in the file picker

## Selecting a file

Browse your Drive folders and select the file you want to transcribe. Click **Select** to add it to your order.

## Permissions

Qualtranscribe only requests read access to your Drive files — we cannot modify or delete anything in your Google Drive.

<Note>
  You only need to connect Google Drive once. Your connection will be remembered for future orders.
</Note>
