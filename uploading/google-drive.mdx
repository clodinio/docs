title: "Import from Google Drive"
description: "How to connect Google Drive and import audio or video files for transcription without downloading them to your device."
---

## Connecting Google Drive

1. From the upload panel, click **Google Drive**
2. Sign in with your Google account when prompted
3. Grant Qualtranscribe read access to your Drive files
4. Your Drive will open in the file picker

You only need to connect Google Drive once. Your connection is remembered for future orders.

## Selecting a File

Browse your Drive folders and select the recording you want to transcribe. Click **Select** to add it to your order.

## Permissions

Qualtranscribe requests read-only access to your Google Drive files. We cannot modify, delete, or create any files in your Drive.

<Note>
  If your Google Drive recording is very large or stored in a shared drive with restricted access, the import may require additional time or permissions. Contact support if you encounter issues.
</Note>
