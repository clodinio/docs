# Smart Insights

Smart Insights is Qualtranscribe's built-in transcript viewer. Once a transcription order is completed, you can open the transcript directly in your browser — no downloading required.

## Opening a transcript

1. Go to **Transcripts** in the sidebar
2. Find your completed transcript
3. Click **Open in Smart Insights**

The transcript will open in a clean, readable view with speaker labels, timestamps, and speaker turns clearly formatted.

## What Smart Insights shows

- Full transcript text, formatted by speaker turn
- Speaker labels (where included in your order)
- Timestamps (where included in your order)
- Language toggle (for translated transcripts)

## Who can use Smart Insights

Smart Insights is available to all Qualtranscribe users — both prepaid and WorkHub accounts. Any completed Human Transcription or Instant Draft can be opened in Smart Insights.
