## Opening a Transcript

1. Go to **Transcripts** in the sidebar
2. Find your completed transcript
3. Click **Open in Smart Insights**

The transcript opens in a clean reading view with speaker labels, timestamps, and speaker turns clearly formatted.

## Layout

Smart Insights displays your transcript in speaker-turn format. Each turn shows:

- The **speaker label** (e.g., Interviewer, Respondent 1, Moderator, Witness) if speaker labels were included
- The **timestamp** at the start of the turn if timestamps were requested
- The full **transcript text** for that turn

## Navigating Long Transcripts

For long transcripts, timestamps every 2 minutes allow you to locate specific sections quickly and cross-reference with your original recording. Use your browser's search function (Ctrl\+F or Cmd\+F) to find specific words or phrases throughout the transcript.

## Running Smart Insights Analysis

From within the transcript view, you can run Smart Insights to generate thematic analysis, speaker patterns, research memos, a quotes bank, and a Coding CSV for qualitative software. See [Smart Insights Overview](/insights/overview) for the full list of outputs.

## Editing Your Transcript

You can edit your transcript directly within Smart Insights. Click any section of the transcript to make changes inline.

## Audio Sync

Smart Insights syncs your transcript with the original audio. Click any passage in the transcript to jump directly to that point in the recording. This makes it easy to verify accuracy, review context, and navigate long recordings without scrubbing through the audio manually.

## Downloading Your Transcript

Download options are available from the **Transcripts** section of your dashboard. Available formats include:

- DOCX (Microsoft Word)
- PDF
- SRT (subtitle format with timestamps)
- Plain text
- Timestamped formats
- Markdown
- NVivo-compatible formats (Synchronized, Headings, Basic)

## Transcript Not Appearing

If you open Smart Insights and the transcript is not loading:

1. Refresh the page
2. Confirm the order status is **Completed** in your Orders section
3. Check that you are viewing the correct order
4. If the issue persists, contact [support@qualtranscribe.com](mailto:support@qualtranscribe.com) with your order number
