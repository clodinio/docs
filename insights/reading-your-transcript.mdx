# Reading Your Transcript

## Layout

Smart Insights displays your transcript in a speaker-turn format. Each turn shows:

- The **speaker label** (e.g. Interviewer, Respondent 1) if speaker labels were included
- The **timestamp** at the start of the turn if timestamps were included
- The **transcript text** for that turn

## Navigating long transcripts

For lengthy transcripts, scroll through the document. Timestamps every 2 minutes (if ordered) help you locate specific sections quickly and cross-reference with your original recording.

## Downloading your transcript

To download the transcript file, go back to the **Transcripts** section and use the download option on the relevant order. Smart Insights is for reading — downloads are handled from the Transcripts section.

## Transcript not appearing?

If you open Smart Insights and the transcript isn't showing:

- Refresh the page
- Check that the order status is **Completed** in your Orders section
- If the issue persists, contact [support@qualtranscribe.com](mailto:support@qualtranscribe.com) with your order reference

<Note>
  Smart Insights is a read-only viewer. Editing transcripts is not currently supported in-app.
</Note>
