---

## Profile

Update your display name and email address from the Profile section. If you update your email address, a confirmation will be sent to the new address before the change takes effect.

## Saved Payment Methods

Manage your saved cards from the Billing section of your settings. Saved cards are stored and managed by Stripe. You can add, update, or remove saved cards at any time.

## Notifications

Manage email notification preferences from Settings. Available notifications include:

- Order confirmation
- Transcript delivery
- Revision completion
- WorkHub team activity (WorkHub accounts)

## Subscription Management

View your current plan, usage, and renewal date from the Subscription section. To upgrade or change your plan, select the appropriate option from the Subscription page.

## Data Residency

Your data residency region (United States or European Union) is set at account creation and cannot be changed after activation. If you need to change your region, contact [support@qualtranscribe.com](mailto:support@qualtranscribe.com). Note that changing regions requires a new account and does not transfer existing data.

## Closing Your Account

To close your account, contact [support@qualtranscribe.com](mailto:support@qualtranscribe.com). Upon closure, your files will be deleted within 30 days per our retention policy. Outstanding invoices must be settled before account closure.
