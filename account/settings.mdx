# Account Settings

Access your account settings by clicking your profile in the sidebar and selecting **Settings**.

## Profile

Update your display name and email address from the Profile section.

## Saved payment methods

Manage your saved cards from the Billing section of your settings. You can remove a saved card at any time.

## Notifications

Manage email notification preferences for order confirmations and transcript delivery alerts.
