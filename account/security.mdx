# Security

## Password

Your password is hashed and never stored in plain text. To change your password:

1. Go to **Settings** in the sidebar
2. Select the **Security** tab
3. Enter your current password and your new password
4. Save changes

## Forgot your password?

On the login page, click **Forgot password** and enter your email address. You'll receive a reset link within a few minutes.

## Session security

If you believe your account has been compromised, change your password immediately and contact [support@qualtranscribe.com](mailto:support@qualtranscribe.com).

## Data storage

Your files and transcripts are stored securely in the cloud. Access is restricted to your account only (and your transcriptionist for Human Transcription orders during processing).
