# HIPAA Readiness

Qualtranscribe is built with HIPAA-readiness in mind, making it suitable for healthcare professionals and researchers who handle Protected Health Information (PHI) in audio or video recordings.

## What this means

- Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest
- Access to files is restricted to authorised users only
- Our infrastructure is designed to support HIPAA-compliant workflows

## Business Associate Agreement (BAA)

If your organisation requires a BAA as part of your HIPAA compliance programme, contact us at [support@qualtranscribe.com](mailto:support@qualtranscribe.com) to discuss.

## Your responsibilities

HIPAA compliance is a shared responsibility. As the data controller, your organisation is responsible for:

- Ensuring you have appropriate consent from research participants before recording
- Limiting the PHI included in recordings where possible
- Managing user access to your Qualtranscribe account appropriately

<Warning>
  Qualtranscribe provides HIPAA-ready infrastructure but cannot guarantee compliance for your specific use case. Consult your institution's compliance officer or legal team for guidance on your obligations.
</Warning>
