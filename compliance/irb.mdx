## Why Your IRB Cares About Transcription

When you submit recordings for transcription, you are transmitting participant data to a third party. For IRB-approved research, this creates obligations around:

- Confidentiality: ensuring participant data is handled with the same privacy protections promised in your consent form
- Data security: demonstrating that the third-party processor meets appropriate standards
- AI tool disclosure: newer IRBs are explicitly asking whether AI tools are used to process participant data and under what terms
- Data retention and deletion: confirming files are not retained beyond the period authorized in your protocol

Choosing a transcription provider is not an administrative afterthought. It is a compliance decision.

## What Qualtranscribe Provides for Your IRB Submission

**No AI training on participant data.** Many AI transcription tools use uploaded audio to improve their models. Qualtranscribe does not, on any plan. This satisfies the IRB's concern that participant data might be used for purposes beyond the stated research.

**Documented data handling chain.** We can provide written documentation of every step in our data processing workflow for inclusion in your data management plan.

**BAAs for healthcare-related research.** If your study involves Protected Health Information, a signed Business Associate Agreement is required before data is submitted. We provide BAAs on request.

**DPAs for EU-based research.** For research involving EU participants, a Data Processing Agreement is available.

**Data deletion confirmation.** Files are deleted 30 days after delivery. Earlier deletion is available on request with confirmation. A Certificate of Destruction is available if your protocol requires it.

**US-Only processing option.** For protocols that require domestic-only data handling with no international transfer, US-Only processing limits all work to US-based professionals and US-based infrastructure.

**Audit trails.** All file access is logged. If your IRB or institution requires an audit trail of who accessed participant recordings, this is available.

## Consent Language for Your Consent Form

Your participant consent form should disclose that recordings will be transcribed by a professional service. Suggested language:

> "Your interview will be audio/video recorded. The recording will be transcribed by a professional transcription service operating under a confidentiality agreement with the research team. The transcription service handles all recordings in accordance with \[HIPAA/GDPR, as applicable\] and does not use recordings or transcripts to train AI systems. Recordings will be securely deleted after transcription is complete. Only the research team will have access to your transcript."

Adapt this language to match the specific services you are using (Human Transcription only, or including Instant Draft or Smart Insights).

## Disclosing AI Tools in Your IRB Protocol

If you are using Instant Draft or Smart Insights, your IRB protocol should disclose:

**Instant Draft:** "Audio recordings may be processed by AssemblyAI's automated speech recognition system to produce draft transcripts. AssemblyAI operates under a Business Associate Agreement with Qualtranscribe. Audio is deleted from AssemblyAI's systems upon transcript completion and is not used for model training."

**Smart Insights:** "Completed transcript text may be processed by Anthropic Claude language models, accessed via AWS Bedrock, to generate thematic analysis and research outputs. This processing is covered by a Business Associate Agreement with AWS Bedrock. Content is not retained or used for model training."

If your IRB requires that no AI tools process participant data, select Human Transcription only and do not use Instant Draft or Smart Insights.

## Common IRB Questions About Transcription Services

**"Does the transcription service use uploaded data to train AI models?"** No. Qualtranscribe does not use client data for AI training on any plan or service tier.

**"Where is participant data stored?"** US accounts: Wasabi Technologies, us-east-1 (North Virginia, USA). EU accounts: Wasabi Technologies, eu-central-2 (Frankfurt, Germany). Data never leaves its designated region.

**"Can participant data be permanently deleted on demand?"** Yes. Request deletion via [support@qualtranscribe.com](mailto:support@qualtranscribe.com). Deletion is processed within five business days. Certificate of Destruction available.

**"Do transcriptionists sign confidentiality agreements?"** Yes. All transcriptionists and contractors sign NDAs as a baseline requirement before accessing any files.

**"Is there a BAA for health-related research?"** Yes. Contact [support@qualtranscribe.com](mailto:support@qualtranscribe.com) to request one before submitting any PHI.

**"Is there a documented audit trail of file access?"** Yes. All access events are logged with user ID, timestamp, and action.

**"Does the service support US-only data processing?"** Yes. Specify US-Only when placing your order or contact support to configure it as your account default.
