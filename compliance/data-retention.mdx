# Data Retention and Deletion

## Project Files

Retention periods are configurable from your account settings under **HIPAA & Compliance**. Available options:

| Period | Description |
|---|---|
| 30 days | Default |
| 60 days | |
| 90 days | |
| 180 days | |
| 1 year | |
| Never | Files retained until manually deleted |

Set your retention period before uploading files. For projects with ongoing activity, ensure your retention policy covers the full anticipated project period.

Once your retention period expires, files are permanently deleted. The deletion process:

1. Data removed from active systems
2. Removed from all backups
3. Overwritten using industry-standard secure deletion methods
4. Verified as completely erased

Files are not recoverable after this process is complete.

## Early Deletion

If your protocol, institutional policy, or personal preference requires deletion before your selected retention period, contact support@qualtranscribe.com with your order number and a deletion request. We process early deletion requests within **five business days**.

A **Certificate of Destruction** is available upon request confirming that your files have been permanently deleted. This is accepted by IRBs, compliance teams, and institutional data officers as documentation of deletion.

## Account Records

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|---|---|
| Project files and transcripts | 30 days after delivery |
| Contact and billing information | 7 years (required by tax and legal regulations) |
| Communication records (emails, support) | 1 year |
| Audit logs (file access events) | Retained for compliance purposes |

## AI Subprocessor Deletion

**Instant Draft (AssemblyAI):** Audio submitted for AI transcription is deleted from AssemblyAI's systems upon transcript completion. It is not retained or used for model training.

**Smart Insights (Anthropic via AWS Bedrock):** Transcript text processed for analysis is not retained by Anthropic or AWS Bedrock beyond the processing event. It is not used for model training.

## Deletion on Account Closure

Upon account closure, project files are deleted within 30 days per our standard retention policy. Contact and billing information is retained for the period required by applicable tax and legal regulations. Communication records are retained for 1 year.

## HIPAA Retention Requirements

For HIPAA-covered research, PHI is retained only for the time necessary to fulfill the transcription request and quality assurance, typically no longer than 30 days after delivery. PHI is never retained longer than your specified protocol requires.

For studies with multi-phase data collection, contact support to arrange retention periods that align with your IRB-approved data management plan.

## GDPR Retention Requirements

For GDPR-covered projects, personal data is retained no longer than necessary to fulfill the requested service. The standard 30-day window satisfies the data minimization and storage limitation principles of GDPR. Data subject erasure requests are processed within five business days.

## Requesting Deletion

To request deletion of any files or data before the standard retention period:

Email: support@qualtranscribe.com
Include: your order number or account email, the specific files or data you want deleted, and whether you require a Certificate of Destruction
