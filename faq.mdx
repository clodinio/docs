# Frequently Asked Questions

## Transcription

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What types of transcription do you offer?">
    We deliver accurate, secure, and professionally formatted transcripts for research, legal, and professional use, including interviews, focus groups, depositions, lectures, usability sessions, and multilingual recordings. Whether your project is IRB-approved, HIPAA-sensitive, legally sensitive, or commercially confidential, we tailor our workflow to fit.

    We offer two services:

    - **Human Transcription:** completed by professional transcriptionists delivering the highest accuracy for research, legal, and professional use
    - **Instant Draft:** AI-powered transcription delivered in minutes for quick turnarounds and early-stage review and analysis
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can you handle multiple speakers or overlapping dialogue?">
    Yes. Our transcriptionists are trained to handle complex recordings with crosstalk, interruptions, and layered conversation, whether for focus groups, roundtables, depositions, or multi-party legal proceedings. The Speaker Labels add-on clearly identifies each participant throughout the transcript.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you offer transcription in languages other than English?">
    Yes. We support multilingual transcription and translation across 20\+ languages for Human Transcription, including Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Arabic, Mandarin, Japanese, Swahili, Amharic, Turkish, and Russian. Instant Draft supports transcription in 99\+ languages. If you have non-English content, select your language during the order process or contact us to discuss your requirements.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you use AI in your transcription process?">
    All core transcriptions at Qualtranscribe is completed by trained human transcriptionists, which is critical for research reliability, legal accuracy, and secure data handling. AI-generated transcripts are available via our Instant Draft service if you need rapid turnaround or an early review pass. Both services deliver professionally formatted transcripts with speaker labels and timestamps.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What's the difference between Clean Verbatim and Full Verbatim?">
    **Clean Verbatim** omits unnecessary utterances (um, uh, false starts, filler words) to produce a polished, readable transcript while preserving full meaning and context.

    **Full Verbatim** captures and includes every utterance — filler words, sighs, stutters, repetitions, and non-verbal sounds. Recommended for conversation analysis and discourse analysis.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Privacy & Security

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do you protect my data? And who has access to it?">
    We use encrypted file transfer, secure storage, and strict access controls. Only the assigned transcriptionist(s) and project manager handling your specific project have access to your files. We never share or reuse any client content.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do you handle Personally Identifiable Information (PII)?">
    All recordings and transcripts are handled as strictly confidential. PII is never shared, reused, or retained beyond the agreed retention period. We offer anonymisation and redaction services upon request, and all transcriptionists operate under signed NDAs.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are your services HIPAA-compliant?">
    Yes. We offer HIPAA-compliant workflows for projects involving Protected Health Information (PHI), including encrypted file handling, secure storage, and strict access controls. We provide signed Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) upon request. Contact [support@qualtranscribe.com](mailto:support@qualtranscribe.com) to request a BAA or discuss your compliance requirements.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Using the App

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need to install anything?">
    No. Qualtranscribe is entirely browser-based. Sign up at [app.qualtranscribe.com](https://app.qualtranscribe.com) and start uploading files immediately.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What file formats do you support?">
    We support the most common audio and video formats including MP3, MP4, WAV, M4A, and WebM. See the full [Supported File Formats](/transcription/supported-formats) page for details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I import files from cloud storage?">
    Yes. You can import directly from Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, Box, Zoom, and Webex. Connect your account during the upload flow and browse your files directly.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is WorkHub?">
    WorkHub is a secure, folder-based workspace for postpaid and institutional clients. It allows you to organise recordings and transcripts into projects and receive invoices rather than paying per upload. Contact [support@qualtranscribe.com](mailto:support@qualtranscribe.com) to set up a WorkHub account.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is Smart Insights?">
    Smart Insights is Qualtranscribe's built-in transcript viewer. Once your transcription is complete, you can open and read your transcript directly in the browser — with speaker labels, timestamps, and speaker turns clearly formatted. No downloading required.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
