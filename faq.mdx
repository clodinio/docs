# Frequently Asked Questions

## Transcription

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What types of transcription do you offer?">
    We specialise in academic, clinical, and market research transcription, including interviews, focus groups, lectures, usability sessions, and multilingual recordings. Whether your project is IRB-approved, HIPAA-sensitive, or commercially confidential, we tailor our workflow to fit.

    We offer two services:
    - **Human Transcription** — completed by professional transcriptionists for publication-quality accuracy
    - **Instant Draft** — AI-powered transcription delivered in minutes for quick turnarounds and early-stage analysis
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can you handle multiple speakers or overlapping dialogue?">
    Yes. Our transcriptionists are trained to handle complex recordings with crosstalk, interruptions, and layered conversation — ideal for focus groups and roundtables. The Speaker Labels add-on clearly identifies each participant throughout the transcript.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you offer transcription in languages other than English?">
    Yes. We support multilingual transcription and translation across 20+ languages, including Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Arabic, Mandarin, Japanese, Swahili, Amharic, Turkish, and Russian. If you have non-English content, select your language during the order process or contact us to discuss your requirements.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do you use AI in your transcription process?">
    All core Human Transcription at Qualtranscribe is completed by trained human transcriptionists — which is critical for research reliability and secure data handling. AI-generated drafts are available via our **Instant Draft** service if you need rapid turnaround or an early directional pass. Both services deliver analysis-ready formatting, speaker labels, and timestamps.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What's the difference between Clean Verbatim and Full Verbatim?">
    **Clean Verbatim** omits unnecessary utterances (um, uh, false starts, filler words) to produce a polished, readable transcript while preserving full meaning and context.

    **Full Verbatim** captures and includes every utterance — filler words, sighs, stutters, repetitions, and non-verbal sounds. Recommended for conversation analysis and discourse analysis.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Privacy & Security

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do you protect my data? And who has access to it?">
    We use encrypted file transfer, secure storage, and strict access controls. Only the assigned transcriptionist(s) and project manager handling your specific project have access to your files. We never share or reuse any client content.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do you handle Personally Identifiable Information (PII)?">
    We treat all recordings and transcripts as confidential. PII is never reused or stored beyond the agreed retention period. We offer anonymisation and redaction upon request.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are your services HIPAA-compliant?">
    Yes. We offer HIPAA-compliant workflows for projects involving Protected Health Information (PHI). This includes encrypted file handling, secure storage, and signed Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) upon request. Contact [support@qualtranscribe.com](mailto:support@qualtranscribe.com) to request a BAA.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Using the App

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need to install anything?">
    No. Qualtranscribe is entirely browser-based. Sign up at [app.qualtranscribe.com](https://app.qualtranscribe.com) and start uploading files immediately.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What file formats do you support?">
    We support the most common audio and video formats including MP3, MP4, WAV, M4A, and WebM. See the full [Supported File Formats](/transcription/supported-formats) page for details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I import files from cloud storage?">
    Yes. You can import directly from Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, Box, Zoom, and Webex. Connect your account during the upload flow and browse your files directly.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is WorkHub?">
    WorkHub is a secure, folder-based workspace for postpaid and institutional clients. It allows you to organise recordings and transcripts into projects and receive invoices rather than paying per upload. Contact [support@qualtranscribe.com](mailto:support@qualtranscribe.com) to set up a WorkHub account.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is Smart Insights?">
    Smart Insights is Qualtranscribe's built-in transcript viewer. Once your transcription is complete, you can open and read your transcript directly in the browser — with speaker labels, timestamps, and speaker turns clearly formatted. No downloading required.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
