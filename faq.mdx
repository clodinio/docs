# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is Qualtranscribe?">
    Qualtranscribe is a transcription platform built for qualitative researchers. We offer both human transcription (delivered by professional transcriptionists) and Instant Draft (AI-powered, delivered in minutes).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Who is Qualtranscribe designed for?">
    We primarily serve academic researchers, UX researchers, market researchers, consultants, and healthcare professionals who need accurate, well-formatted transcripts for qualitative analysis.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need to install anything?">
    No. Qualtranscribe is entirely browser-based. Just sign up and you can start uploading files immediately.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Transcription

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What's the difference between Human Transcription and Instant Draft?">
    **Human Transcription** is completed by a professional transcriptionist and is ideal when you need high accuracy, speaker labels, verbatim formatting, or publication-ready output.

    **Instant Draft** uses AI to transcribe your file within minutes. It's useful for quick reviews, early-stage analysis, or when turnaround speed is the priority.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What file formats do you support?">
    We support most common audio and video formats including MP3, MP4, WAV, M4A, and WebM. If you're unsure whether your format is supported, please contact support before uploading.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What languages do you support?">
    We support a wide range of languages for both Human Transcription and Instant Draft. Language selection is available during the order process. If you need a specific language not listed, contact our support team.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What does 'Full Verbatim' mean?">
    Full Verbatim means the transcript captures everything spoken, including filler words (um, uh, like), false starts, repetitions, and non-verbal sounds (laughter, sighs). Clean Verbatim removes these and produces a tidier, more readable output.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I get speaker labels in my transcript?">
    Yes. The **Speaker Labels** add-on is available for Human Transcription orders. Each speaker will be clearly labelled throughout the transcript (e.g. Interviewer, Respondent 1).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Uploading

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I import files from cloud storage?">
    Yes. You can import directly from Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, Box, Zoom, and Webex. Connect your account in the upload flow and browse your files directly.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is there a file size limit?">
    Please contact support if you're working with very large files (over 2GB) as handling may differ from standard uploads.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Billing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does billing work?">
    Prepaid users pay at the point of upload via Stripe using a credit or debit card. Postpaid and institutional clients are invoiced manually through WorkHub.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my payment information secure?">
    Yes. All payments are processed through Stripe. Qualtranscribe never stores your card details directly.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I get a refund?">
    Please contact our support team if you have concerns about an order. Refund eligibility depends on the status of the transcription at the time of the request.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Privacy & Security

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is Qualtranscribe HIPAA-ready?">
    Yes. Qualtranscribe is built with HIPAA-readiness in mind, making it suitable for healthcare professionals handling patient interview data. Contact us if you require a BAA (Business Associate Agreement).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Who has access to my files?">
    Your files are private to your account. For Human Transcription orders, files are accessed by our transcription team solely for the purpose of completing your order. WorkHub folders are only accessible to authorised users within your account.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## WorkHub

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is WorkHub?">
    WorkHub is a secure, folder-based workspace for postpaid and institutional clients. It allows you to organise recordings and transcripts into projects, manage team access, and receive invoices rather than paying per upload.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I get access to WorkHub?">
    WorkHub access is set up manually. Contact our team at [support@qualtranscribe.com](mailto:support@qualtranscribe.com) to discuss your requirements.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
