# Welcome to Qualtranscribe

Qualtranscribe is a professional transcription service built for research, legal, and professional use, whether you're conducting in-depth interviews, focus groups, ethnographic fieldwork, usability studies, or handling depositions, client interviews, and legal proceedings.

We offer two transcription services:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Human Transcription" icon="user" href="/transcription/human-transcription">
    Professional transcriptionists deliver accurate, formatted transcripts with speaker labels, verbatim options, and timestamps.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Instant Draft" icon="bolt" href="/transcription/instant-draft">
    AI-powered transcription delivered in minutes. Ideal for quick turnarounds and early-stage analysis.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Who is Qualtranscribe for?

- **Academic researchers** conducting interviews and needing publication-ready transcripts
- **UX researchers** running usability sessions and think-aloud studies
- **Market researchers** transcribing focus groups and consumer interviews
- Legal professionals handling depositions, hearings, and client interviews
- **Consultants** managing large volumes of qualitative data
- **Healthcare professionals** handling patient interviews with HIPAA-readiness in mind

## Key features

- Upload from your device, Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, Box, Zoom, or Webex
- Choose Human Transcription or Instant Draft per file
- Add-ons: verbatim formatting, speaker labels, timestamps, and translation
- **WorkHub** — a secure folder workspace for teams, institutions, and high-volume clients across research, legal, and professional services
- **Smart Insights** — AI-powered transcript analysis and insights for every use case, directly in-app
