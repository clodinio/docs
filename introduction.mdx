# Qualtranscribe Documentation

Qualtranscribe is a professional transcription and translation platform built for organizations that handle sensitive recordings, confidential interviews, and critical documentation. We serve research institutions, healthcare systems, law firms, market research agencies, universities, public health teams, and enterprise operations groups across more than 40 countries.

This documentation covers everything you need to operate the platform confidently: uploading files, ordering transcription, managing projects through WorkHub, working with translations, using Smart Insights for AI-assisted analysis, and understanding our security and compliance frameworks.

## What Qualtranscribe Provides

**Human Transcription** delivers 99%\+ accuracy by trained professional transcriptionists. It is the appropriate choice for depositions, clinical interviews, focus groups, academic research, regulatory documentation, and any project where precision is not negotiable.

**Instant Draft** uses AI-powered speech recognition to return a verbatim transcript within minutes of upload. It covers 99\+ languages and is well suited to high-volume workflows, preliminary review, and projects where speed takes priority.

**Translation Services** convert audio directly to English transcript via native-speaking bilingual transcriptionists, or produce both a source-language transcript and an English version as a two-step process. We support 20\+ language pairs for human transcription and 99\+ languages for AI transcription.

**Smart Insights** is an AI-powered analysis layer built for qualitative research and legal transcription. Running directly within Smart Insights, it generates a structured Summary, Key Moments, Interview Analysis, Thematic Analysis, Research Synthesis, Q&A Pairs, Contradictions, Research Notes, and a Quotes Bank. For legal transcripts, it produces a Witness Summary, Event Timeline, Key Concessions, Non-Answers Log, Key Statements Index, and Contradiction Flags. An AI Chat function allows you to ask questions directly across your transcripts. This is available for AI-generated transcripts (Instant Draft) only. 

**WorkHub** is a secure folder-based workspace for teams, institutions, and organizations managing ongoing or high-volume transcription work. It supports project-level organization, team collaboration, and invoiced billing arrangements.

## Platform Architecture

Qualtranscribe operates across two account types:

**Prepaid accounts** pay per order at checkout via Stripe. You upload a file, configure your order, and pay before work begins. Suitable for individual researchers, solo practitioners, and organizations with variable transcription needs.

**WorkHub accounts** are configured for teams and institutions with ongoing requirements. Files are organized into projects and folders, teams collaborate within a shared workspace, and billing is handled through invoiced arrangements rather than per-order payments.

## Security and Compliance

All data is encrypted at rest using 256-bit AES and in transit using TLS 1.3. File storage is regionally isolated: US accounts use Wasabi's us-east-1 region (North Virginia) and EU accounts use Wasabi's eu-central-2 region (Frankfurt, Germany). Files are permanently deleted 30 days after project delivery, with earlier deletion available on request.

Qualtranscribe is HIPAA-ready with Business Associate Agreements available for healthcare and clinical research clients. We are GDPR-compliant with Data Processing Agreements available for EU-based operations. All transcriptionists and contractors operate under signed Non-Disclosure Agreements.
