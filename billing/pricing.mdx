## Human Transcription

Human Transcription is priced per audio minute.

| Service | Rate |
| --- | --- |
| In-depth interviews (IDIs) | \$1.00 per audio minute |
| Focus groups | \$1.50 per audio minute |
| Bilingual direct transcription (audio to English) | \$6.00 per audio minute |

Pricing is based on the length of your recording, not word count or page count. The total shown at checkout includes your base transcription rate plus any add-ons and turnaround selections.

## Add-on Pricing

Add-ons are priced at checkout and applied to the base rate.

- **Timestamps**: N/A
- **Full Verbatim** (vs. Clean Verbatim): \+\$0.30 due to increased transcription time
- **Rush delivery** (12-24 hours): higher surcharge

## Translation Services

| Service | Rate |
| --- | --- |
| Bilingual direct transcription (Spanish, French, and major languages) | \$6.00 per audio minute |
| Focus group translation | \$6.00 per audio minute |

The bilingual direct approach delivers English output directly from the source audio without producing an intermediate source-language transcript. It costs significantly less and is appropriate when you only need the English version. The two-step process is required when you need both the source-language transcript and the English translation.

## Instant Draft Subscription Plans

Instant Draft is included in subscription plans.

| Plan | Monthly AI minutes | Price |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Free | 75 minutes | \$0 |
| Pro | 1,200 minutes | \$18/month |
| Unlimited | Unlimited minutes | \$35/month |

Annual billing is available at a 30% discount: Pro at $12.60/month, Advanced at $24.50/month.

Pro and Advanced plans also include:

- Human Transcription discounts (5% on Pro, 10% on Advanced)
- Multilingual Instant Draft support (99\+ languages)
- GDPR-secure processing and HIPAA-compliant transcription
- Expanded Smart Insights access (150 insight runs on Pro, unlimited on Advanced)
- AI chat across transcripts (20 transcripts on Pro, unlimited on Advanced)
- Full export suite including SRT, Markdown, DOCX, and timestamped formats
- Zoom, Teams, GoToMeeting, and Webex integrations

## Academic and Institutional Discounts

We offer a 5-10% discount on Human Transcription for students, faculty, and grant-funded research projects. Institutional purchase orders and direct departmental billing are available for organizations with ongoing needs.

Contact [support@qualtranscribe.com](mailto:support@qualtranscribe.com) with your project details to discuss pricing.
