# Pricing

Qualtranscribe pricing is based on the length of your audio or video file and the service selected. Prices are displayed at checkout before you submit payment.

## Prepaid accounts

Prepaid users pay per order at the point of submission via Stripe. The total shown at checkout includes:

- Base transcription cost (calculated per minute of audio)
- Any add-ons selected (verbatim format, timestamps, speaker labels, translation)
- Next-day delivery surcharge (if selected)

## WorkHub accounts

WorkHub clients are invoiced manually. Pricing is agreed when your WorkHub account is set up. Contact [support@qualtranscribe.com](mailto:support@qualtranscribe.com) to discuss rates.

## Promo codes

If you have a promo code, you can apply it at the payment step in the order flow. Discounts are applied to the order total before payment.

<Note>
  All prices shown at checkout are final. No hidden fees are added after submission.
</Note>
