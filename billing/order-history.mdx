# Order History

All your transcription orders are listed in the **Orders** section of your dashboard.

## What you can see

For each order you can view:

- Order reference number
- Service type (Human Transcription or Instant Draft)
- File name
- Order date
- Current status
- Estimated or actual delivery date
- Total paid

## Viewing order details

Click any order to open the full detail view. From here you can:

- See the complete order breakdown including add-ons
- View the delivery date
- Access the completed transcript (for completed orders)

## Finding a specific order

Orders are listed in reverse chronological order. If you need to find a specific order, use your order reference number from your confirmation email.

## Receipts and invoices

For prepaid orders, a receipt is sent to your registered email address after each successful payment. If you need a copy, contact [support@qualtranscribe.com](mailto:support@qualtranscribe.com) with your order reference.
