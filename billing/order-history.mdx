# Order History

All transcription orders are listed in the **Orders** section of your dashboard, in reverse chronological order.

## What You Can See

For each order you can view:

- Order reference number
- Service type (Human Transcription or Instant Draft)
- File name and length
- Order date and delivery date
- Current status
- Total charged
- Add-ons included

## Order Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Processing** | Your order is with the transcription team |
| **Completed** | Your transcript is ready |
| **Revision in Progress** | A revision request is being processed |

## Accessing Your Transcript

Click any completed order to access the transcript. From the order detail view you can:

- Open the transcript in Smart Insights
- Download in your preferred format
- View the full order breakdown including add-ons
- Initiate a revision request if needed

## Receipts

A receipt is sent to your registered email address after each successful payment. If you need a copy, contact support@qualtranscribe.com with your order reference number.

For WorkHub and institutional accounts, itemized invoices are provided at your agreed billing interval. Contact support for copies of specific invoices.

## Finding a Specific Order

Orders are listed newest first. Use your order reference number from your confirmation email to locate a specific order quickly. For large order histories, the reference number is the most reliable identifier.
