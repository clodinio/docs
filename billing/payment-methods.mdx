# Payment Methods

## Credit and debit cards

Qualtranscribe accepts all major credit and debit cards via **Stripe**, our payment processor. Accepted cards include Visa, Mastercard, and American Express.

Your card details are handled entirely by Stripe — Qualtranscribe never stores your card information directly.

## Saved cards

After your first order, you can save your card for faster checkout on future orders. Saved cards are stored securely by Stripe and can be removed from your account settings at any time.

## WorkHub invoicing

WorkHub accounts do not pay per order. Instead, work is invoiced manually at agreed intervals. See [Invoicing](/workhub/invoicing) for details.

## Payment security

All transactions are processed over HTTPS. Stripe is PCI-DSS compliant. Your payment data is never transmitted to or stored on Qualtranscribe's servers.
