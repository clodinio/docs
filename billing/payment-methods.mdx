## Prepaid Accounts

Prepaid orders are paid at checkout via Stripe. Accepted methods include:

- Major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- ACH bank transfers

Qualtranscribe does not store your card details. All card data is handled by Stripe, which is PCI-DSS compliant. You may save a card for faster checkout on future orders; saved cards are managed through Stripe and can be removed from your account settings at any time.

## WorkHub and Institutional Accounts

WorkHub accounts support:

- Institutional purchase orders (Net 30 payment terms)
- ACH bank transfers
- Wire transfers (international clients)
- Direct invoicing to departmental or grant accounts

To apply for institutional billing terms or arrange purchase order payment, contact [support@qualtranscribe.com](mailto:support@qualtranscribe.com).

## Payment Security

All transactions are processed over HTTPS. Card data never passes through Qualtranscribe servers. Stripe is PCI-DSS Level 1 certified, the highest level of compliance available.

## Refund Processing Times

| Payment Method | Refund Timeline |
| --- | --- |
| Credit/Debit Card (Stripe) | 5-10 business days |
| PayPal | 3-5 business days |
| Wire Transfer | 10-15 business days (may incur fees) |
| Purchase Order | Credit memo within 5-7 business days |
