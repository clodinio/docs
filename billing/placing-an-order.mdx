# Placing an Order

## Step by step

1. Sign in to your account at [app.qualtranscribe.com](https://app.qualtranscribe.com)
2. Click **New Transcription** from your dashboard
3. Upload your file or import from a connected cloud service
4. Select your service: **Human Transcription** or **Instant Draft**
5. For Human Transcription: choose your add-ons and turnaround window
6. Review your order summary
7. Enter payment details and click **Submit Order**

## Order confirmation

After submitting, you'll receive an email confirmation with your order reference number. Keep this for your records.

## Tracking your order

Go to **Orders** in the sidebar to view the status of all your orders. Statuses include:

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Processing** | Your order has been received and is being worked on |
| **Completed** | Your transcript is ready in the Transcripts section |
| **Incomplete** | Your order was not fully submitted — please contact support |

## Order notes

At checkout you can add notes to your order. Use this to provide context for your transcriptionist — speaker names, technical terminology, challenging audio sections, or anything else that will help them produce an accurate transcript.
