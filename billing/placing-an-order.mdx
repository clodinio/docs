# Placing an Order

## Step by Step

1. Sign in to your account at [app.qualtranscribe.com](https://app.qualtranscribe.com)
2. Click **Order Transcription** from your dashboard
3. Upload your file directly or import from Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, Box, Zoom, or Webex
4. Select your service: **Human Transcription** or **Instant Draft**
5. For Human Transcription: select your transcript format (Clean or Full Verbatim), add-ons, and turnaround
6. Add any order notes to provide context for your transcriptionist
7. Review your order summary
8. Complete payment and submit

## Order Notes

The order notes field is available at checkout for Human Transcription orders. Use it to provide:

- Speaker names and roles
- Technical terminology, specialized vocabulary, or a glossary
- Subject matter context (the topic of the interview, the study focus)
- Known audio challenges (background noise, accents, recording quality)
- Formatting preferences not covered by the standard options
- Special handling requirements (anonymization, redaction)

Clear order notes are one of the most effective ways to improve transcript quality, particularly for specialized content.

## Order Confirmation

After submitting, you will receive an email confirmation with your order reference number. Keep this for your records. It is required for revision requests and billing inquiries.

## Order Status

Go to **Orders** in the sidebar to view the status of all your orders.

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Processing** | Your order has been received and is being worked on |
| **Completed** | Your transcript is ready in the Transcripts section |
| **Revision in Progress** | A revision has been submitted and is being processed |

## WorkHub Orders

For WorkHub accounts, transcription orders are initiated from within the relevant project folder. Completed transcripts are returned to the same folder. There is no per-order checkout; all work is billed through your invoiced arrangement.
