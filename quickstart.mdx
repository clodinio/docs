# Quickstart

This guide walks you through placing your first transcription order from sign-up to receiving your transcript.

## Step 1 : Create your account

Go to [app.qualtranscribe.com](http://app.qualtranscribe.com) and sign up with your email address. You'll receive a confirmation email, click the link to activate your account. During sign-up, select your preferred **data residency region** to ensure your data is stored in compliance with your local regulations.

<Note>
  Already have an account? [Sign in here](https://app.qualtranscribe.com/login).
</Note>

## Step 2 : Upload your file

**Prepaid:** From your dashboard, click **Order Transcription**. Upload a file directly from your device or import from a connected cloud service: Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive, Box, Zoom, or Webex. Supported formats include MP3, MP4, WAV, M4A, and WebM.

**WorkHub:** Navigate to **WorkHub**, create a folder or project, and start uploading your files directly into your workspace.

## Step 3: Choose your service

Select the transcription service that fits your needs:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Human Transcription" icon="user">
    Best for publication-quality accuracy. Delivered by a professional transcriptionist within your chosen turnaround window.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Instant Draft" icon="bolt">
    AI-powered. Ready in minutes. Ideal for quick reviews or when speed matters more than perfection.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Step 4: Choose your add-ons

For Human Transcription orders you can add:

| Add-on | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| **Full Verbatim** | Includes all filler words, false starts, and non-verbal sounds |
| **Timestamps** | Inserts a timestamp every 2 minutes |
| **Speaker Labels** | Labels each speaker (e.g. Interviewer, Respondent 1) |
| **Translation** | Translates the transcript into English |

## Step 5: Pay and submit

Review your order summary, enter your card details, and submit. You'll receive an email confirmation with your order reference.

## Step 6: Receive your transcript

Once complete, you will receive an email notification once your transcript is ready.

**Human Transcription:** View your transcript directly in Office Online or download it to your device.

**Instant Draft:** Open your transcript in Smart Insights to edit, review, analyze, and extract insights directly in-app.

**WorkHub:** You will receive an email notification when your completed transcript has been uploaded to your workspace.

<Check>
  That's it. Most Instant Draft orders are ready within minutes. Human Transcription turnaround times depend on the option selected at checkout.
</Check>
