# Market Research Transcription

Qualtranscribe supports market research agencies, insights teams, brand strategy groups, and research firms with accurate, analysis-ready transcription for the full range of qualitative research methods. Pricing starts at $1.00 per audio minute for IDIs and $1.50 per minute for focus groups.

## What We Transcribe

**In-depth interviews (IDIs).** One-on-one consumer interviews, stakeholder conversations, and expert sessions. $1.00 per audio minute.

**Focus groups.** Multi-speaker discussions with speaker identification, handling crosstalk, overlapping dialogue, and complex group dynamics. $1.50 per audio minute.

**Usability testing and UX research.** User feedback sessions, think-aloud studies, prototype reactions, and interface testing. Every hesitation, task comment, and spontaneous reaction captured.

**Concept testing.** Consumer responses to product concepts, messaging directions, and creative approaches, transcribed with the emotional texture that makes the reaction analytically useful.

**Shop-alongs and ethnographic sessions.** In-context consumer recordings, retail behavior observations, and naturalistic research environments.

**Mobile diaries and video ethnography.** Voice notes, video entries, and asynchronous participant submissions organized into structured transcripts.

**Online panels and virtual focus groups.** Zoom, Teams, and Webex recordings handled with the same accuracy as in-person sessions.

**Mystery shopping reports.** Audio recordings from mystery shoppers transcribed for cross-location service analysis.

## Why Accuracy Matters in Market Research

Consumer insights work runs on tight timelines and client deadlines. When a transcript misses a key phrase or misattributes a comment to the wrong speaker, the error travels forward into your analysis, your coding, your report, and ultimately your client's strategic decision-making.

Qualtranscribe uses human transcriptionists for all research work. For consumer data involving regional accents, multilingual participants, product-specific terminology, or heavy group dynamics, human transcription consistently outperforms AI on accuracy.

## NVivo and Analysis Tool Compatibility

Transcripts are formatted for direct import into NVivo, ATLAS.ti, MAXQDA, Dedoose, and Dovetail. Speaker labels, timestamps, and paragraph formatting are structured to work with each tool's import requirements. Specify your QDA software at checkout.

## Smart Insights for Insights Teams

Smart Insights generates thematic maps, speaker pattern analysis, quotes banks organized by theme, research memos, and a deck outline from completed Instant Draft transcripts. For agencies running multiple concurrent projects, this accelerates the path from transcript to debrief without replacing the interpretive work that makes qualitative research valuable.

Consumer research often involves unreleased products, competitive positioning, or proprietary strategic direction. All research content is handled under strict confidentiality. Transcriptionists sign NDAs as a baseline requirement. No content is shared, discussed, or retained beyond the project.
