## What We Transcribe

**Dissertation and thesis interviews.** Verbatim transcripts formatted for NVivo, ATLAS.ti, or committee review. IRB-compliant handling as standard.

**In-depth interviews.** Phenomenological research, grounded theory studies, ethnographic interviews, and exploratory qualitative investigations.

**Focus group discussions.** Multi-speaker transcription with speaker identification, handling overlapping dialogue, moderator questions, and group dynamics across participant sets of any size.

**Oral history projects.** Community recordings, life story interviews, archival testimonies, and heritage documentation. Dialect, regional speech patterns, and cultural references handled with care.

**Fieldwork recordings.** Field interviews, community consultations, participant observation recordings, and ethnographic audio from research environments worldwide.

**Clinical and medical research interviews.** IRB-approved patient research, clinical trial interviews, and behavioral health studies with HIPAA-compliant workflows.

**Multilingual academic research.** Transcription in 20\+ languages, translation to English, and bilingual formatting for cross-language analysis.

**Lectures and seminars.** Recorded teaching sessions, conference presentations, and research seminars.

## IRB Compatibility

Qualtranscribe is built to work within IRB-approved data management frameworks. We provide:

- Written documentation of our security practices for IRB data management plan submissions
- Business Associate Agreements for research involving PHI
- Data Processing Agreements for EU participant data
- No-AI-training confirmation letters
- US-Only processing for protocols prohibiting international data transfer

See [IRB Compliance](/compliance/irb) for consent language templates and IRB documentation available on request.

## NVivo and ATLAS.ti Formatting

Transcripts are available pre-formatted for direct import into qualitative analysis software:

- **NVivo Synchronized:** Timestamps synced with audio for media playback within NVivo
- **NVivo Headings:** Organized by interview question for auto-coding
- **NVivo Basic:** Clean text import compatible with all NVivo versions
- **ATLAS.ti, MAXQDA, Dedoose:** Available on request

Specify your QDA software when placing your order.

## Smart Insights for Academic Research

Smart Insights generates preliminary thematic analysis, speaker pattern breakdowns, research memos, a coded quotes bank, and a Coding CSV for NVivo from your completed Instant Draft transcripts. For dissertation researchers managing 15-20 interviews, this reduces the time between completing fieldwork and beginning substantive analysis.

Smart Insights is a starting point for your analytical work, not a substitute for it. All outputs should be interrogated against your own reading of the data.

## Turnaround for Academic Projects

Standard turnaround is 3-5 business days. For dissertation timelines and conference deadlines, expedited (24-48 hours) and rush (12-24 hours) options are available.

For large batches of interviews, we offer rolling delivery so you can begin analysis on completed transcripts while remaining files are still in progress. Contact support to arrange rolling delivery for your project.
