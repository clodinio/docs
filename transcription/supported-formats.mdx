# Supported File Formats

Qualtranscribe accepts the most common audio and video formats used in research and fieldwork.

## Supported formats

| Format | Type | Common use case |
|---|---|---|
| **MP3** | Audio | Interview recordings, voice memos |
| **MP4** | Video | Screen recordings, video interviews |
| **WAV** | Audio | High-quality field recordings |
| **M4A** | Audio | iPhone and Mac voice recordings |
| **WebM** | Video/Audio | Browser-based recordings, Zoom exports |

## Cloud-imported formats

When importing from Zoom or Webex, recordings are typically delivered in MP4 or M4A format and are fully supported.

## Tips for best audio quality

- Record in a quiet environment with minimal background noise
- Use an external microphone where possible for field interviews
- Keep the recorder close to all speakers, especially in focus groups
- Avoid recording near fans, air conditioning units, or open windows

## File size

For very large files (over 2GB), please contact our support team before uploading as handling may differ from standard uploads.

<Note>
  If your file is in a format not listed above, contact [support@qualtranscribe.com](mailto:support@qualtranscribe.com) before uploading.
</Note>
