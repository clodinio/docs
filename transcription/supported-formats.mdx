## Accepted Formats

| Format | Type | Common Sources |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **MP3** | Audio | Voice recorders, phone apps, interview tools |
| **MP4** | Video | Zoom recordings, screen captures, video interviews |
| **WAV** | Audio | Field recorders, professional audio equipment |
| **M4A** | Audio | iPhone voice memos, Mac recordings, Zoom audio-only |
| **WebM** | Video/Audio | Browser-based recordings, Google Meet exports |

## Cloud-Imported Files

When importing recordings from Zoom, Webex, Google Meet, or Teams, files are typically delivered in MP4 or M4A format. Both are fully supported.

## File Size

For files larger than 2GB, contact [support@qualtranscribe.com](mailto:support@qualtranscribe.com) before uploading. Handling for very large files may differ from standard uploads and may affect turnaround estimates.

## Audio Quality and Accuracy

The quality of your recording directly affects transcript accuracy. Our transcriptionists can work with challenging audio, but the following conditions improve outcomes significantly:

**Recording environment:**

- Record in a quiet room with minimal ambient noise
- Avoid rooms with hard surfaces and high ceilings that cause echo
- Close windows and doors; keep HVAC units switched off during recording if possible

**Equipment:**

- Use an external microphone rather than a built-in laptop or phone microphone
- For focus groups and multi-participant sessions, use boundary microphones placed centrally or lapel microphones per participant
- Test your setup with a short recording before your full session

**During the recording:**

- Ask participants to speak one at a time where possible
- Have participants introduce themselves at the start of the recording
- If conducting a video interview, ask participants to use headphones to reduce echo

**For field recordings:**

- Position your recorder 30-60cm from the primary speaker
- Shield your microphone from wind if recording outdoors
- Use a recorder with a wind filter or furry cover for outdoor fieldwork

## Recording in Multiple Languages

For multilingual recordings or sessions where participants switch between languages:

- Note the languages present in your order notes
- Indicate whether you need the transcript in the source language, in English, or both
- Provide context about who speaks which language if known

For highly localized dialects or rare language variants, include this context in your order notes so we can match your recording to an appropriately experienced transcriptionist.

## Formats Not Listed

If your recording is in a format not listed above, contact [support@qualtranscribe.com](mailto:support@qualtranscribe.com) before uploading. We can advise on conversion options or confirm compatibility.
