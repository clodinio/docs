## How It Works

1. Upload your file and select **Instant Draft**
2. Select your language from the 99\+ supported options
3. Submit your order (no payment required beyond your subscription)
4. Your transcript is processed automatically and returned within minutes
5. Access it in the Transcripts section under **Instant Drafts**, or open directly in Smart Insights

## What Instant Draft Includes

- Automatic speaker detection where audio permits
- Timestamps at speaker turns
- Clean verbatim output
- 99\+ language support
- Direct integration with Smart Insights for AI-powered analysis

## Language Coverage

Instant Draft supports major world languages where AI accuracy is strongest, including Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Mandarin, Arabic, Italian, Japanese, Russian, Korean, Dutch, Polish, Turkish, Hindi, and many more. It also covers a broad range of additional languages including Swahili, Amharic, Bengali, Tagalog, Vietnamese, Thai, Indonesian, Ukrainian, Romanian, Hebrew, and others.

For lower-resource languages or recordings with heavy regional accents, accuracy will vary. Human Transcription is recommended for these cases.

## Accuracy Considerations

Instant Draft performs well on:

- Clear recordings with minimal background noise
- Standard accents and general vocabulary
- Single speaker or clearly separated speakers
- Interviews and conversations in supported high-resource languages

Accuracy is reduced by:

- Heavy background noise or low recording volume
- Strong regional accents or non-standard dialects
- Domain-specific terminology (medical, legal, highly technical)
- Heavy crosstalk or simultaneous speakers
- Lower-resource languages with limited training data

An independent study by the CISPA Helmholtz Center confirmed that AI transcription consistently produces meaning-distorting errors on technical terminology. For research, legal, or clinical content where terminology precision matters, Human Transcription is the appropriate service.

<Warning>
  Instant Draft transcripts should be reviewed before use in published research, legal proceedings, regulatory submissions, or formal reporting. They are not reviewed by a human transcriptionist unless you separately purchase a human review upgrade.
</Warning>

## Deleting an Instant Draft

Instant Draft transcripts can be deleted from the Instant Drafts tab in your Transcripts section. Deleted transcripts are permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

Audio submitted to AssemblyAI for Instant Draft processing is deleted from AssemblyAI's systems upon transcript completion. Neither Qualtranscribe nor AssemblyAI uses your audio to train AI models.

## When to Use Instant Draft

Instant Draft is well suited for:

- Preliminary review of a large batch of recordings before commissioning human transcription
- High-volume workflows where a first pass is needed quickly
- Early-stage thematic scanning before deeper analysis
- Transcripts destined for Smart Insights analysis where AI will process the output
- Internal notes, informal research tasks, and non-sensitive content
- Projects where speed is the primary requirement and precision can be reviewed afterward

## When to Use Human Transcription Instead

Choose Human Transcription when:

- The transcript will be used in legal proceedings or regulatory submissions
- Your project is IRB-approved with human subjects data
- You are working with clinical, healthcare, or sensitive patient content
- The recording has technical terminology, heavy accents, or poor audio conditions
- The transcript will be directly cited in published or academic work
