# Instant Draft

Instant Draft uses AI to transcribe your audio or video file and deliver a readable transcript within minutes. It is designed for speed — useful when you need a quick first pass at your content before deeper analysis or before commissioning a Human Transcription.

## How it works

1. Upload your file and select **Instant Draft** as your service
2. Pay via Stripe and submit
3. Your transcript is processed automatically
4. The completed draft appears in the **Transcripts** section under the **Instant Drafts** tab

## What Instant Draft includes

- Automatic speaker detection (where audio permits)
- Automatic timestamps
- Clean verbatim output

## Limitations

Instant Draft is AI-powered and will not match the accuracy of a human transcriptionist in all cases. It may struggle with:

- Heavy accents or non-standard dialects
- Multiple overlapping speakers
- Low-quality audio or significant background noise
- Domain-specific terminology (medical, legal, academic)

<Warning>
  Instant Draft transcripts should be reviewed before use in published research or formal reporting. For publication-quality output, use Human Transcription.
</Warning>

## Deleting an Instant Draft

You can delete an Instant Draft from the Instant Drafts tab in your Transcripts section. Deleted drafts are permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

## When to use Instant Draft

- Quick content review before a research session debrief
- Checking whether a recording is usable before ordering a full human transcript
- Early-stage thematic scanning
- Internal notes and informal research tasks
