# Human Transcription

Human Transcription is completed by professional transcriptionists who review your audio or video file and produce a formatted, accurate transcript. It is the recommended service for qualitative research, academic work, and any project requiring publication-ready output.

## How it works

1. Upload your file and select **Human Transcription** as your service
2. Choose your add-ons (verbatim format, timestamps, speaker labels, translation)
3. Select your turnaround window
4. Pay via Stripe and submit
5. Receive your completed transcript in the **Transcripts** section of your dashboard

## Add-ons

You can customise your transcript with the following add-ons at checkout:

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Full Verbatim">
    Captures everything spoken — filler words (um, uh, like), false starts, repetitions, and non-verbal sounds such as laughter or sighs. Recommended for conversation analysis and discourse analysis.

    The default format is **Clean Verbatim**, which removes these elements for a tidier, more readable output.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Timestamps">
    Inserts a timestamp every 2 minutes throughout the transcript. Useful for navigating long recordings and cross-referencing with your audio.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Speaker Labels">
    Each speaker is clearly identified throughout the transcript (e.g. Interviewer, Respondent 1, Respondent 2). Recommended for focus groups, paired interviews, or any multi-speaker recording.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Translation">
    Translates your transcript into English. Useful when your recording is in another language and you need an English-language output for analysis or reporting.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Turnaround times

Turnaround options are displayed at checkout based on file length and current capacity. Options typically include standard and next-day delivery windows.

<Note>
  Turnaround times are calculated from the point of order confirmation, not upload time.
</Note>

## Transcript delivery

Completed transcripts are delivered to your **Transcripts** section. You can:

- Read the full transcript in **Smart Insights**
- Download the transcript file
- View the associated order details

## Tips for best results

- Ensure your recording has minimal background noise
- If you have multiple speakers, brief your transcriptionist in the notes field at checkout
- For domain-specific terminology (medical, legal, academic), include a glossary or key terms in the order notes
