## Human Transcription

Professional transcriptionists complete your transcript from start to finish. No AI tools access your audio for standard human transcription orders. Human transcription is the appropriate choice when:

- The transcript will be used in legal proceedings, regulatory submissions, or formal documentation
- Your project is IRB-approved and involves human subjects data
- You are working with clinical, healthcare, or sensitive patient content
- Accuracy is non-negotiable and the recording involves technical terminology, multiple speakers, or challenging audio conditions
- The transcript will be cited directly in published academic or research work
- You require verbatim precision for conversation analysis or discourse research

## Instant Draft

AI-powered transcription delivered in minutes. This is the right choice when:

- You need a quick first pass before deeper analysis
- Turnaround speed matters more than perfection
- You're working with clear, single-speaker recordings
- You want to review content before commissioning a human transcript

## Choosing the right service

|  | Human Transcription | Instant Draft |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Accuracy | 99%\+ guaranteed | High for clean audio, variable for complex recordings |
| Turnaround | 3-5 business days standard | Minutes |
| Speaker labels | Add-on | Automatic |
| Verbatim options | Full or Clean | Clean only |
| Timestamps | Add-on | Automatic |
| Translation | Add-on (20\+ languages) | Available (99\+ languages) |
| Smart Insights | Not available | Available |
| Legal proceedings | Recommended | Preliminary review only |
| Clinical and healthcare | Recommended | Use with caution |
| IRB-approved research | Recommended | Requires IRB protocol review |
| AI training on your data | Never | Never |
