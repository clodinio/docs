## Instant Draft

Instant Draft orders are processed automatically. Most files are returned within a few minutes of submission. Processing time scales with file length; a 90-minute recording may take slightly longer than a 30-minute one.

## Human Transcription

| Turnaround | Window | Additional Fee |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Standard | 3-5 business days | None |
| Expedited | 24-48 hours | Yes |
| Rush | 12-24 hours | Yes |

Turnaround begins from the point of order confirmation and payment receipt, not from file upload time.

## Large and Bulk Projects

For projects involving a high volume of files, turnaround is assessed individually by your project manager based on total audio volume, language requirements, audio complexity, and current capacity. A confirmed turnaround schedule is provided before work begins.

Transcripts are delivered on a rolling basis as files are completed, so your team can begin working with early transcripts while the remainder of the project is still in progress. Contact [support@qualtranscribe.com](mailto:support@qualtranscribe.com) before placing a large order to discuss scheduling and delivery arrangements.

## What Can Extend Turnaround

The following conditions may extend the delivery window beyond the stated timeframe:

- Poor audio quality requiring additional review time
- Files longer than 2 hours
- Very high volumes of overlapping speech
- Specialized terminology requiring additional research
- Exceptionally high order volume during peak periods

## Delivery

Completed transcripts appear in the **Transcripts** section of your dashboard. You will receive an email notification when your transcript is ready. Instant Draft transcripts can be opened directly in Smart Insights. Human Transcription transcripts are available for download in your preferred format.

Files are retained in your account for 30 days after project delivery. After 30 days they are permanently deleted per our data retention policy. Download your transcripts within this window.

<Note>
  For large projects with multiple files, we can arrange rolling delivery so your team can begin analysis on completed transcripts while remaining files are still in progress. Contact support to arrange this.
</Note>
