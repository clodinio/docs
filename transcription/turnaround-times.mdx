# Turnaround Times

## Instant Draft

Instant Draft orders are processed automatically and are typically ready within a few minutes of submission. Exact processing time depends on file length.

## Human Transcription

Turnaround options for Human Transcription are displayed at checkout. The options available to you depend on your file length and current transcriptionist capacity.

Options typically include:

- **Standard** — delivered within a set number of business days
- **Next Day** — delivered by the next business day (available as an add-on)

<Note>
  Turnaround times begin from the point your order is confirmed and payment is received — not from the time of upload.
</Note>

## Delivery

Once your Human Transcription is complete, your transcript will appear in the **Transcripts** section of your dashboard. You will also receive an email notification.

## Delays

In rare cases, orders may take longer than the stated turnaround if:

- Audio quality is significantly below standard
- The file contains very high volumes of overlapping speech
- Current order volume is unusually high

If your order is delayed beyond the estimated delivery window, please contact [support@qualtranscribe.com](mailto:support@qualtranscribe.com) with your order reference.
